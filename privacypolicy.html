Website:


This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website. By using our site, you agree with this policy.

1. Data We Collect

When you log in or use certain features, we collect and store the following data:

  • Discord ID (e.g., 947868777528307742)
  • Discord Username (e.g., pgnetwork01)
  • Discord Display Name (e.g., Pg Network)
  • Discord Avatar (profile picture)
  • Verified Status (whether your Discord account is verified)
  • First Login Timestamp
  • Last Login Timestamp
  • Login Count
  • Xbox Username (if connected, e.g., NetworkPg)
  • Xbox Verified Status

All of this data is stored securely in Cloudflare storage and also in your browser cookies. All data stored in Cloudflare and in cookies is automatically deleted every 7 days.

Note: Your Discord profile picture, display name, and connections (such as Xbox) are not considered private information by Discord and are visible to anyone on Discord. If you have something private in your Discord connections and do not want it to be shown publicly, please remove it from your Discord account before using our site. We are not responsible for any information you choose to make public via your Discord connections.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your real name, address, or phone number for analytics or tracking purposes. We do not track user activity or use certain analytics tools that link data to you to gather browsing data. We use a privacy-focused analytics tool that does not collect personal data or track individual users. This means we do not store or analyze your browsing history, search queries, or any other personal information that could identify you. This is used to track how many visits we get, what devices are used, and what pages are visited. We do not use this data to create user profiles or track individual behavior across the site. None of this data is linked to you

3. Cookies & Local Storage Usage

We use cookies to store your login session and user preferences securely in your browser. This is required for authentication and to provide a personalized experience. We also use localStorage to remember your preferences. This data is stored on your device and is never shared with third parties by us. All cookie data is deleted every 7 days.

4. Google AdSense

This website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. We use this to gain income to pay for extra services we use to keep the website and AI running. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. These cookies help deliver relevant ads and measure their effectiveness. You can learn more about how Google uses data here.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy policies. Please review their terms before using them.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with the updated date.

7. Contact

If you have any questions, you can reach us via the contact section of our website, through Discord, or by email at craftersmcguides@gmail.com.

CraftersMC AI:

1. Prompts

CraftersMC AI is a separate service that uses an open-source AI on together-ai, which has been modified to create our own AI model. None of us (together-ai and craftersmc guides) store this. All that happens is we send your prompt to our own API, then after processing and anonymizing your prompt we send it to together.ai, which sends it to our custom model there to provide AI-generated responses. It does not collect or store any personal information from users. The AI does not have access to your Discord or Xbox usernames, and it does not track user activity. Prompts are deleted after processing, and no user data is stored or used for training purposes.


2. Responses

The AI is designed to respect user privacy and confidentiality. It does not retain conversations or use them for training purposes. All interactions with the AI are processed in real-time and are not stored. All our training data is from the CraftersMC Wiki, which is publicly available, or from the CraftersMC Guides team, which is our own brains. The AI does not have access to any private data or personal information about users.
