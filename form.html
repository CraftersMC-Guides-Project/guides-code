Frequently Asked Questions

A Data Skimmer is a community member who helps collect, update, and verify in-game data for the project, ensuring information is accurate and up-to-date for everyone.

No, you do not need to know coding languages. The main requirement is attention to detail and willingness to help with data collection and updates.

There is no strict minimum, but you should be able to contribute updates every 2–7 days to stay consistent with the team's needs.

Data is submitted through forms or directly to the team leads, following the guidelines provided after joining the team.

Perks include recognition in the community, early access to some features, and the satisfaction of helping others. There is no monetary compensation.

No, Data Skimmers are not official staff members, but they are trusted contributors with a special role in the community.
