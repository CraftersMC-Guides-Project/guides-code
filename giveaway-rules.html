Giveaway Rules

  1. Only join if you can claim your prize.
  2. Hosts only and can choose to cancel or reroll the giveaway if you don’t claim your prize.
  3. You cannot claim from friends or alt accounts/profiles, only from which you joined the giveaway.
  4. Joining without claiming 3-4 times in a row may result in being removed from future giveaways.
  5. If you don’t claim within the specified time, the giveaway will be rerolled, and hosts can set the next timing based on the rerolls.
  6. Users facing real-life issues will be given extra time to claim their prizes.
  7. Winners can only claim from their original profiles (the first one you created that is still in existence).
  8. Sponsors may take time to reward you, winners can contact admins if the host is not replying from a long period of time (i.e. 24-48h+).
  9. Winners cannot ask sponsors to give their rewards to others; the prize will be rerolled or canceled instead.
  10. Winners have the right to report sponsors for any scams; so speak for yourself to the admins.
  11. If the giveaway has issues, admins can restart the giveaway.
  12. Begging winners or host for prizes may lead to disqualification from future giveaways.
