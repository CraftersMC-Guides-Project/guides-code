Giveaway Rules

  1. Participants must be members of the CraftersMC Discord server at the time of the giveaway.
  2. One entry per user is allowed unless stated otherwise.
  3. Multiple accounts or bot entries are strictly prohibited and will lead to disqualification.
  4. Winners will be selected randomly using a secure method.
  5. Winners must respond within 48 hours of announcement to claim their reward.
  6. Prizes are non-transferable and cannot be exchanged unless specified.
  7. Staff members may not be eligible for all giveaways. Check individual giveaway notes.
  8. Participants must follow all community rules and remain respectful to others.
  9. The organizers reserve the right to change rules or cancel giveaways without prior notice.
  10. By participating, you agree to these rules and any additional terms mentioned in the giveaway details.
