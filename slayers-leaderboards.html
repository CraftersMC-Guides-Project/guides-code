What Once Was: A Legacy Remembered
The first leaderboard page was originally created by NumberOne230564 and David_R06 using Google Docs, which was quite basic. Later, CraftersMC Guides improved it by designing a cleaner page and using information from that original Google Docs site and the page was created for actually NumberOne230564. However, NumberOne230564 chose not to work with us and eventually created his own page because he wanted no involvement of "CraftersMC Guides" certainly. Since we didn’t want to erase the time and effort that went into this project, we decided to keep it instead of deleting it. This is a shoutout to their early work, and a clarification that we didn’t collect all of the initial data ourselves. At present, NumberOne230564 and his team manage a separate page, while we run this page independently and collect our own data ourselves.
