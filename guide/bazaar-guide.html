The Bazaar guide


The Bazaar is an in-game trading system where players can buy and sell materials directly with others. You no longer need to spam chat or wait for buyers/sellers—just list your items or place an order!


How to Access the Bazaar

To unlock the Bazaar, you need to have an average skill level of 7 (this includes Farming, Mining, Combat, Foraging, and other skills except taming). Once you meet this requirement, you can access the Bazaar in the following ways:

  • Option 1: Talk to Flick in the Village Center.
  • Option 2: Use the Bazaar-book in the Village Center.
  • Option 3: (For Diamond/Emerald ranks): Use '/bazaar' or '/bz'.


What Can You Trade?

  • Farming Items: Crops like wheat, potatoes, etc.
  • Mining Items: Resources like emerald, diamonds.
  • Combat Items: Mob drops.
  • Foraging Items: Wood.
  • Oddities: Rare or unique materials.


Main Features


1. Sell Offers

List your items for a price you choose.
Steps:
1. Select an item and amount.
2. Choose your price (lower prices fill faster).
3. Wait for a buyer or use the "instant sell" option if you're in a hurry.


2. Buy Orders

Place an order for items at a price you specify.
Steps:
1. Select an item and amount.
2. Set your price (higher prices fill faster).
3. Wait for a seller or use the "instant buy" option for quick transactions.


3. Instant Sell

Quickly sell items to existing buy orders.
Note: You’ll get 4% fewer coins upfront to account for price changes, but the full amount will be refunded after the transaction.


4. Instant Buy

Quickly buy items from existing sell offers.
Note: You’ll pay 4% extra upfront to account for price changes, but the extra will be refunded after the transaction.


Extra Features

  • Average Price: View the 7-day average price of any item to avoid overpaying or underselling.
  • Rank Perks:
    • Diamond/Emerald ranks: Use Bazaar remotely with '/bazaar' or '/bz'.
    • Emerald rank: Mark your favorite items for quick access and use shortcut commands (e.g '/buy wheat 64' will instantly buy 64 wheat).

Bazaar Trading Strategies


1. Profit from Price Fluctuations

Bazaar Flipping: Buy low and sell high. Use graphs to identify price dips or spikes. Example: Buy Enchanted Rotten Flesh at a low price and create a sell offer at a higher rate.


2. Bulk Orders for Discounts

Create buy orders for large quantities instead of buying instantly. This allows you to get items at a lower cost.


3. Focus on High-Demand Items

Items like the Super Compactor 3000 often have steady demand and good margins.


4. Use Presets for Efficiency

Utilize preset quantities and pricing to save time while creating orders or offers.


5. Avoid Overpricing

Keep your offers competitive by matching or slightly undercutting existing prices.


How to Fix Bazaar Errors

This guide solves two common Bazaar (BZ) problems and shows how to avoid unnecessary taxes.


Problems

  1. "Not Enough Supply" Error
    • Sometimes, the BZ says there isn’t enough supply even when it shows listings.
  2. Hidden Taxes on Insta-Buys
    • When insta-buying, the price might be higher than expected due to taxes (e.g., buying something listed for 10k might cost 11k).

Solution

  1. Insta-Buy a Single Unit
    • Buy 1 unit of the item to check its availability.
  2. Check Sell Orders
    • After buying 1 unit, go to Sell Orders to see all active listings.
  3. Create a Custom Buy Order
    • Find the listing you want (e.g., 100 units at 100k).
    • Copy the quantity and price into your buy order.
    • This method ensures you buy the item instantly without taxes.

This approach lets you buy items directly while avoiding extra taxes and errors.


How to Unlock the Bazaar
in Just One Day as a New Player


To access the Bazaar, you need to reach Level 7 in Farming, Mining, Foraging, Combat, and Enchanting. Follow these steps to achieve this within a day:


1. FARMING:

  • Purchase a Rookie Hoe from the Farming NPC.
  • Head to Harvesting Heaven and farm wheat until you reach Farming Level 7.

2. MINING


  • Obtain a Zombie Pickaxe by crafting or purchasing one.
  • Travel to the Coal Mine and mine coal until you reach Mining Level 7.

3. FORAGING


  • Craft or purchase a Jungle Axe for efficient wood collection.
  • Teleport to the Oak Forest and chop wood until Foraging Level 3 to unlock The Park.
  • Once unlocked, go to the Dark Oak Forest (inside The Park) and continue gathering Dark Oak Wood until you reach Foraging Level 7.

4. COMBAT


  • Place 5-6 Tier 1 Zombie Minions on your island.
  • These minions will generate enough resources within a day to help you reach Combat Level 7.

5. ENCHANTING


  • Craft or purchase an Enchanting Table and bookshelfs.
  • Use the table to enchant your Zombie Pickaxe with Efficiency V.
  • Remove the enchantment and repeat the process until you reach Enchanting Level 7.

By following these steps, you can unlock the Bazaar in just one day and start trading efficiently!
