CraftersMC Starter Guide

Written by Ieduoo for CraftersMC Guides

A new journey begins!


  • Farm wheat in Harvest Haven.
    • Craft yourself a set of Farm Armor.
    • Grind for enchanted pumpkins and sell them in the Bazaar.
    • Continue farming until you earn 600k–800k Coins.

Next Steps:


  • Buy a Personal Compactor and build a cobblestone generator.
  • Grind cobblestone until you obtain a Rock Pet, then sell it to the Oliver NPC for 2.6m+ Coins.

Advance Further:


  • Build a Pumpkin Farm on your island.
  • Start grinding pumpkins while using an Epic or Legendary Bee Pet, which you can purchase from the Buzz NPC.
  • Craft yourself a pair of Farmer Boots.

Congratulations! You've progressed from a starter to a intermediate player. No more begging!
